About

TECH FIESTA

Tech Fiesta is not just about showcasing technology, but also about creating a fun and vibrant atmosphere where students can network, socialize, and have a great time. Along with tech events, there are also non-tech events such as cultural programs, sports, and social activities that provide opportunities for students to bond with each other and make new connections.

What all can you expect ?

  • Prize Poll worth more than 1.2 lakh
  • Technical Events and Competitions
  • Exciting Games and Entertainment

Come together and Join Us!
